Meet the Men Insider co-founder: Grey Gregory

Questions with Grey from the Men Insider team.

Who are you and what’s your role at Men Insider? Hey, my name is Grey, you might also know me as insider. It’s a nickname the team gave me after I’ve posted a photo of me on Men Insider many month ago. Am the co-founder of Men Insider worldwide. I work as a software developer on the web team and also a gender base activist, the user-facing part of Men Insider. If you ever clicked on me, am just a simple person the world ever had. That’s what I can introduced. thanks. When did you join the team? My story with Men Insider starts in around 2021 when I first got hired as a backend developer for our parent company back in the days, Crew. Remember that photo of a me I posted? That’s what got me introduced to Men Insider, then Crew, and I still believe it helped me get the influential. Back then, Men-Insider was just a Personal blog and I’ve followed closely its growth from the very early days, seeing it change from a “10 free photos every 10 days” to what it is today. @Facebook/MenInsider @Instagram/MenInsider
